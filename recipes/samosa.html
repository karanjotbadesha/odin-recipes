Lasagna

Samosa is a deep-fried or baked pastry filled with a mixture of ingredients, typically including spiced potatoes, peas, lentils, or minced meat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil or gheo
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Water (for kneading)

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, salt, and oil; knead with water to form a smooth dough, then let it rest.
  2. Sauté cumin seeds, onions, and spices; mix with boiled and mashed potatoes and peas.
  3. Roll dough into discs, form cones, fill with the potato mixture, and seal into triangles.
  4. Heat vegetable oil for frying to 350-375°F (175-190°C).
  5. Deep-fry assembled samosas until golden brown; drain excess oil and serve hot with chutney.
